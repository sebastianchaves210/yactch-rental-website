Your Miami Yacht Experience Starts Here

Scroll to discover

Miami Yacht Collective started with one frustration: booking a yacht in Miami had become a circus of platforms, brokers, and service fees stacked on service fees. You’d send an inquiry on Friday night and hear back Monday afternoon — by which point the date was already gone.

So we built something simpler. A small, hand-picked fleet — from a 28′ Axopar sport boat to a 90′ Ferretti superyacht — out of one dock on the Miami River. When you call, you talk directly to the team that handles your charter from inquiry to dock-back.

Our captains know Biscayne Bay the way most people know their neighborhood. Star Island, Nixon’s Sandbar, Stiltsville — routed however the day calls for it. The boats are clean, the crew shows up early, and the music gets loud only when you want it to.

Miami Yacht Collective is a premier yacht rental company based in Miami Beach. We make luxury accessible — whether you’re planning a birthday celebration, bachelorette party, corporate outing, or a sunset cruise with friends. Every charter includes a licensed captain, fuel, ice, and a Bluetooth sound system.
